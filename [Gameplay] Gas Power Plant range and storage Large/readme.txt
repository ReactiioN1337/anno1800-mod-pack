Increases the range and material storage of the Gas Power Plant to 250 and 25 respectively.

**************************************************<README>**************************************************

This mod is made to be used with Spice It Up v2.5
Drop this in the mods folder found here by default: C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Anno 1800\mods
If you have changed this location, you likely know what you're doing and aren't even reading this readme.

To disable this mod, rename the folder to have a "-" as the first character, without quotes.
